Exam Office Contact Information

Purpose
This document explains the role of the Exam Office and the types of questions students can direct there.

Role of the Exam Office
The Exam Office supports the administration and communication of exam-related processes at Nova Tech College.

Examples of Exam Office Topics
Students may contact the Exam Office about:
- exam schedules
- resit procedures
- exam absence reporting
- administrative exam questions
- formal exam-related requests
- review and inspection planning

When Not to Contact the Exam Office
Questions about teaching content, grading explanation, or course-specific study materials should usually be directed first to the relevant teacher.

Communication
Students should use the official school contact channel for exam-related questions and provide identifying information where required.

Response Time
Response times may vary depending on the academic calendar, deadlines, and exam periods.

Contact
Current contact details and opening information can be found on the official student portal.
