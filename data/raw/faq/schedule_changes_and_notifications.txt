Schedule Changes and Notifications

Purpose
This document explains how schedule changes are communicated at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students are responsible for checking their official timetable and school communication channels regularly.

Possible Schedule Changes
Changes may include:
- classroom changes
- teacher replacement
- lesson cancellation
- rescheduled assessments
- updated project sessions
- online instead of on-campus activities

Communication Channels
Schedule changes may be communicated through:
- the student portal
- official school email
- timetable application
- announcements from teachers or departments

Student Responsibility
Students must:
- check updates daily
- read official messages carefully
- respond to urgent schedule changes where required
- avoid relying only on informal messages from classmates

Short-Notice Changes
In some situations, schedule changes may happen on short notice due to illness, technical problems, or organizational needs.

Contact
Questions about schedule changes should be directed to student administration or the relevant department.
