Printing and Copying Rules

Purpose
This document explains the rules for printing and copying at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Printing and copying facilities are provided for educational use and should be used responsibly.

Allowed Use
Printing and copying may be used for:
- study materials
- assignments
- project documents
- official school-related purposes

Responsible Use
Students are expected to:
- avoid unnecessary printing
- use printing resources efficiently
- follow any technical instructions
- respect shared equipment and queues

Restrictions
Printing may be limited during technical issues, maintenance, or high-demand periods. Personal or excessive non-educational printing may be restricted.

Costs and Credits
If a print credit system applies, students are responsible for monitoring their available balance and following the applicable top-up or usage rules.

Contact
Questions about printing and copying should be directed to the ICT helpdesk or library service point.
