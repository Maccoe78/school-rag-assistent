Service Desk Opening Hours

Purpose
This document explains the opening hours and availability of the main student service desks at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students can contact school service desks during official opening hours for administrative, technical, and student support questions.

Main Service Points
The main service points may include:
- student administration desk
- ICT helpdesk
- Exam Office
- internship coordination desk
- library service point

Opening Hours
Opening hours may differ by department and may change during holidays, exam periods, or special school events. Students are expected to check the official portal for the most current schedule.

Outside Opening Hours
Questions sent outside opening hours are handled on the next available working day unless otherwise stated.

Peak Periods
During registration weeks, exam periods, or deadline periods, response times may be longer than usual.

Contact
Students should consult the official portal or front desk information for current opening hours and contact details.
