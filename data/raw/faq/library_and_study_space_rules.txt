Library and Study Space Rules

Purpose
This document explains the rules for using the library and study spaces at Nova Tech College.

General Principle
Library and study spaces are intended to support focused study, research, and academic work.

Expected Behavior
Students must:
- keep noise at an appropriate level
- respect quiet zones
- use study spaces responsibly
- leave shared areas clean
- follow staff instructions

Use of Facilities
Depending on the location, students may have access to:
- books and learning materials
- computers
- printers
- reservation-based study rooms
- silent study areas

Reservations
Reserved spaces must be used according to the applicable booking rules. Repeated no-shows may affect future reservations.

Food and Drink
Rules on food and drink may vary by area. Students must follow local instructions.

Contact
Questions about library use should be directed to the library service point.
