Holiday and Closure Schedule

Purpose
This document explains how holiday periods and school closures are handled at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Official school holidays, closure days, and study breaks are communicated through the official academic calendar.

Possible Closure Types
Closures may include:
- national holidays
- seasonal vacation periods
- study days
- staff training days
- exceptional closure due to unforeseen circumstances

Student Responsibility
Students must:
- check the official calendar
- avoid making assumptions based on previous years
- verify whether internship obligations continue during school holiday periods
- monitor messages about any unexpected closure changes

Important Note
Not all school services operate during closure periods. Some digital systems may remain available while physical services are closed.

Contact
Questions about holidays and closures should be directed to student administration.
