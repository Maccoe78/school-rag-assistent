Online Learning FAQ

Purpose
This document provides general guidance for students participating in online learning at Nova Tech College.

What Online Learning May Include
Online learning may involve:
- live digital classes
- recorded instruction
- discussion boards
- assignment platforms
- remote supervision meetings
- online presentations

Student Expectations
Students are expected to:
- log in on time
- follow instructions from teachers
- participate actively where required
- use respectful online communication
- complete assignments by the stated deadlines

Technical Preparation
Students should:
- test internet and device access in advance
- keep login details available
- know how to access the required platforms
- contact support if technical issues persist

Online Conduct
Students must behave respectfully in digital learning spaces and avoid disruptive, inappropriate, or abusive communication.

Support
If a student experiences ongoing technical or participation problems, the student should contact the teacher, academic mentor, or ICT helpdesk depending on the issue.

Contact
Questions about online learning should be directed to the relevant teacher or ICT helpdesk.
