Learning Platform Access

Purpose
This document explains how students access the online learning platform at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students receive access to the official learning platform after successful registration and account activation.

Platform Use
The learning platform may be used for:
- course materials
- assignment submission
- deadlines
- teacher announcements
- feedback
- learning activities

Login Requirements
Students must use their official school credentials to log in. Access may depend on active enrollment status.

Access Problems
If a student cannot access the learning platform, the student should first:
- check login details
- verify internet connection
- confirm account activation status
- review any maintenance messages

If the issue continues, the student should contact the ICT helpdesk.

Student Responsibility
Students are expected to check the learning platform regularly and use it as an official source of course information.

Contact
Questions about platform access should be directed to the ICT helpdesk.
