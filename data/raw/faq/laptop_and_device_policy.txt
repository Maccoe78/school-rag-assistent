Laptop and Device Policy

Purpose
This document explains the rules for using laptops and other devices at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students are expected to bring and use suitable devices where required for their program activities.

Acceptable Use
Devices may be used for:
- classwork
- assignment completion
- software development
- presentations
- access to school platforms
- educational communication

Student Responsibility
Students are responsible for:
- bringing a charged and functional device where required
- installing required educational software
- handling devices safely and responsibly
- following teacher instructions during lessons and assessments

Restrictions
Device use may be limited when:
- it disrupts the lesson
- unauthorized tools are used during assessments
- the activity requires full attention without digital distraction

School Equipment
If the school provides equipment, students must use it according to school instructions and return it in proper condition where applicable.

Contact
Questions about device use should be directed to the ICT helpdesk or academic mentor.
