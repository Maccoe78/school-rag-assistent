Internship Contract Rules

Purpose
This document explains the rules regarding internship agreements and formal approval at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
An internship may only start officially once the required agreement has been reviewed and approved by the school and all required parties have signed where necessary.

Required Documents
Depending on the internship type, the required documents may include:
- internship agreement
- placement details
- learning objectives
- contact information of the workplace supervisor
- risk or safety information where relevant

Signature Requirement
The agreement may require signatures from:
- the student
- the internship provider
- the school representative

No Approval, No Official Internship
If the required agreement is missing or incomplete:
- the internship may not count toward the program
- insurance or supervision arrangements may not apply
- the student may be required to delay the internship start

Changes During Internship
Any major change in internship conditions must be reported, including:
- change of supervisor
- change of location
- change of working hours
- early termination

Contact
Questions about internship agreements should be directed to the internship coordinator.
