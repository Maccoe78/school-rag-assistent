Sick During Internship

Purpose
This document explains what students must do if they become ill during their internship.

General Rule
If a student is unable to attend the internship due to illness, the student must inform both the internship provider and the school as soon as possible.

Reporting Procedure
The student must:
- notify the workplace supervisor directly
- follow any absence procedure used by the internship provider
- inform the school contact or internship supervisor if required
- provide updates if the illness lasts longer than expected

Missed Hours
Internship hours missed due to illness usually do not count as completed hours. The student may need to make up the missed time depending on the internship agreement and program requirements.

Longer Illness
If illness continues for an extended period:
- the student should contact the school supervisor
- a plan may be discussed for continuation, delay, or recovery
- the internship may need adjustment or extension

Improper Reporting
Failure to report illness correctly may be treated as unauthorized absence from internship.

Contact
Questions about illness during internship should be directed to the internship coordinator.
