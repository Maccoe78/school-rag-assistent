Internship Completion Requirements

Purpose
This document explains the requirements for successfully completing an internship at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
An internship is only considered completed when all required components have been fulfilled and approved.

Completion Requirements
Students must normally complete:
- the minimum required internship hours
- all required internship assignments
- any required reflection or reporting documents
- a satisfactory performance evaluation where applicable
- all formal administrative requirements

Possible Reasons for Non-Completion
An internship may be marked as not completed if:
- required hours are missing
- assignments are incomplete
- the placement ends early without resolution
- performance is insufficient
- required approval documents are missing

Consequences of Non-Completion
If the internship is not completed successfully:
- the student may need to repeat part or all of the internship
- graduation or progression may be delayed
- a recovery plan may be required

Contact
Questions about internship completion should be directed to the internship coordinator.
