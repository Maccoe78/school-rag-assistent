Internship Supervision Roles

Purpose
This document explains the responsibilities of the student, workplace supervisor, and school supervisor during internship.

Student Role
The student is responsible for:
- attending the internship as agreed
- completing tasks and assignments
- asking for clarification when needed
- behaving professionally
- communicating concerns in time

Workplace Supervisor Role
The workplace supervisor is expected to:
- guide the student in day-to-day work
- provide practical feedback
- monitor professional development
- confirm attendance or progress where required
- contact the school if serious issues arise

School Supervisor Role
The school supervisor is expected to:
- monitor educational progress
- support the student when needed
- maintain contact with the internship provider where applicable
- help address learning or supervision concerns
- contribute to assessment when required

Shared Responsibility
Successful internship supervision depends on clear communication and timely reporting of issues by all parties.

Contact
Questions about supervision roles should be directed to the internship coordinator.
