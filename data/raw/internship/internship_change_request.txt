Internship Change Request

Purpose
This document explains how students can request a change of internship placement at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Changing internship placement is only possible when there is a valid reason and when the school agrees that the current placement is no longer suitable or workable.

Possible Reasons
A change request may be considered in cases such as:
- unsafe work environment
- serious conflict
- lack of meaningful learning opportunities
- major changes in internship conditions
- personal circumstances affecting placement suitability

Request Procedure
- The student must explain the reason for the request clearly.
- Relevant evidence or communication history may be requested.
- The school reviews the situation before approving a change.

Important Note
A student may not independently leave or switch internship placement without informing the school, unless immediate safety concerns make this unavoidable.

Consequences
If a placement is changed:
- a replacement internship may need approval
- completed hours may be reviewed
- study delay may occur depending on timing

Contact
Questions about internship changes should be directed to the internship coordinator.
