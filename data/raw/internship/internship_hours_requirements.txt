Internship Hours Requirements

Purpose
This document explains the minimum internship hour requirements at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students must complete the required number of internship hours as defined by the study program in order to successfully finish the internship period.

Hour Registration
- Students must keep an accurate record of completed internship hours.
- The internship provider may be asked to confirm the recorded hours.
- Hours must be completed within the approved internship period unless another arrangement is formally approved.

Incomplete Hours
If a student does not complete the required hours:
- the internship may be marked as incomplete
- additional hours may need to be scheduled
- study progress may be delayed

Absence During Internship
Absence due to illness or other reasons does not automatically count toward internship hours. Missed hours may need to be made up depending on the internship agreement.

Student Responsibility
Students are responsible for:
- tracking attendance
- discussing hour registration with the internship supervisor
- informing the school if hours are at risk

Exceptional Cases
In special situations, the school may review whether an adjusted arrangement is appropriate. This is never automatic and requires formal agreement.

Contact
Questions about internship hours should be directed to the internship coordinator.
