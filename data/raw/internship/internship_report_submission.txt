Internship Report Submission

Purpose
This document explains the rules for submitting internship reports, reflections, and related written work at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students must submit all required internship documents before the stated deadlines and in the format required by the program.

Possible Required Documents
Internship-related submissions may include:
- reflection reports
- weekly logs
- final internship report
- learning objective review
- supervisor feedback forms

Submission Requirements
Students must:
- follow the required format
- submit through the official platform
- use the correct file naming if instructed
- respect the published deadline

Late Submission
Late submission may:
- delay assessment
- lead to an incomplete internship record
- require additional follow-up
- affect study progress

Quality Expectations
Internship reports should show:
- reflection on learning
- professional communication
- connection between practice and study
- clear structure and completeness

Contact
Questions about internship report submission should be directed to the school supervisor or internship coordinator.
