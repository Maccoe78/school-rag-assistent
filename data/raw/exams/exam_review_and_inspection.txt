Exam Review and Inspection
Purpose
This document explains how students can review completed assessments, receive feedback, and request clarification about grading.

Right to Review
Students have the right to review their completed assessment within a reasonable period after publication of the result, unless the nature of the assessment makes this impossible.

Review Options
Review may take place through:

classroom feedback
individual inspection
digital review environment
scheduled feedback meeting
What Students May Review
Students may generally review:

their own answers or submission
the awarded grade or result
feedback comments where available
the applied assessment criteria or answer model where appropriate
Limitations
Students may not:

copy or distribute confidential assessment materials without permission
photograph exam content if this is prohibited
alter reviewed materials
claim access to materials belonging to other students
Request Procedure
Students should request a review within the timeframe communicated by the teacher or Exam Office.
If no timeframe is provided, the student should make the request within 10 working days after publication of the result.
The school may schedule the review at a fixed time and location.
Follow-Up
If a student still disagrees with the result after review:

the student should first discuss the matter with the teacher
if no solution is reached, the student may use the formal objection or appeals procedure
Contact
Questions about assessment review should be directed to the teacher or the Exam Office.
