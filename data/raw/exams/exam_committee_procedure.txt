Exam Committee Procedure
Purpose
This document explains the role of the Exam Committee at Nova Tech College and the procedure for submitting requests or cases.

Role of the Exam Committee
The Exam Committee oversees the quality, fairness, and integrity of formal assessment processes. It handles exceptional requests and reviews cases that fall outside standard procedures.

Matters the Exam Committee May Handle
The Exam Committee may consider:

requests for an additional resit
cases involving fraud or plagiarism
requests related to special circumstances
disputes requiring formal review
exceptions to standard exam regulations
requests for adapted assessment arrangements
When a Student Can Submit a Request
A student may contact the Exam Committee when:

the normal process does not provide a suitable solution
exceptional personal circumstances affect assessment participation
a formal decision is needed on a non-standard case
Submission Procedure
Requests must be submitted in writing through the official school channel.
The request must clearly describe the situation, the desired decision, and any supporting evidence.
Incomplete requests may be delayed or returned for clarification.
Review Process
The committee reviews the submitted information.
Additional information may be requested from the student, teacher, or department.
The committee may consult internal policies before reaching a decision.
Decisions are recorded and communicated to the student.
Decision Timeline
The school aims to respond within a reasonable period, but complex cases may require additional time.

Binding Nature
Decisions of the Exam Committee are formal within the scope of school regulations. If a student disagrees, the appeals and objections procedure may apply.

Contact
Students can find submission instructions and contact details through the Exam Office or student portal.
