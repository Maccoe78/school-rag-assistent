Exam Absence Rules
Purpose
This document describes the rules and consequences related to absence during exams and other formal assessments at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students are expected to attend all scheduled assessments. If a student cannot attend, the absence must be reported correctly and on time.

Valid Reasons for Absence
Absence may be considered valid in cases such as:

illness
medical appointments that could not be scheduled outside exam time
urgent family circumstances
approved special leave
documented technical failure during a remote exam
Invalid Reasons for Absence
Absence is generally considered invalid when:

the student forgot the exam date
the student overslept
travel delays were avoidable
the student failed to report the absence
no supporting explanation is provided when requested
Reporting Procedure
The student must report the absence as soon as possible.
For same-day absence, the student must notify the school before or at the start time of the exam whenever possible.
Reporting must be done through the official reporting channel, such as the student portal, Exam Office, or designated contact address.
Additional supporting documents may be requested.
Consequences of Valid Absence
If the absence is approved:

the assessment attempt may be marked as excused
the student may receive access to a resit or replacement assessment
no misconduct registration will be applied
Consequences of Invalid Absence
If the absence is not approved:

the attempt may be recorded as missed
the student may lose one assessment opportunity
access to a resit may be restricted according to the course rules
During Exam Periods
Students are expected to be available during official exam periods. Personal planning conflicts do not automatically qualify as valid reasons for absence.

Repeated Absence
Repeated absence from assessments may trigger:

a meeting with the academic mentor
additional monitoring
referral to student support services
consequences for study progress
Contact
Questions about exam absence should be directed to the Exam Office.
