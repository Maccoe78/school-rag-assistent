Resit Policy
Purpose
This document explains the rules for resits at Nova Tech College, including eligibility, limits, and request procedures.

General Principle
A resit gives a student another opportunity to complete or improve an assessment result when the original result was insufficient or when participation was missed for an approved reason.

Number of Resits
Students are generally allowed a maximum of two resit opportunities per assessment, unless stated otherwise in a course guide.
Some practical assessments may allow only one resit due to scheduling, resource, or supervision limitations.
Oral assessments may have limited resit options depending on planning capacity.
Eligibility
A student may request or receive a resit if:

the original assessment result was insufficient
the student was absent with an approved reason
the original assessment was declared invalid due to technical or organizational problems
A student may not automatically receive a resit if:

deadlines were missed without approval
the student was absent without reporting according to procedure
the student was excluded due to fraud or misconduct
Resit Request Procedure
Resit requests must be submitted through the student portal or the Exam Office.
Requests must normally be submitted within 10 working days after publication of the result or absence registration.
The student is responsible for verifying whether a resit request is required or whether resits are scheduled automatically.
Planning and Scheduling
Resits are scheduled by the school and communicated through the official timetable or student portal.
The school does not guarantee an individual preferred date or time.
Students must check the schedule themselves and be available at the assigned moment.
Result Registration
The final registered result may be based on the highest grade, the latest grade, or the course-specific regulation, depending on the assessment type.
Course-specific grading rules take precedence if they differ from the general rule.
Additional Resits
An additional resit may only be granted in exceptional cases, such as:

long-term illness
serious personal circumstances
proven technical failure during a digital exam
a formal decision by the exam committee
Limitations
A resit is not guaranteed for every assignment, especially where group work, live presentations, or practical settings make repetition difficult.
Some assignments must be completed within the original teaching period.
Contact
Students with questions about resits should contact the Exam Office or their academic mentor.
