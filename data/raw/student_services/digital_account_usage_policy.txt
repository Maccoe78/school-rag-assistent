Digital Account Usage Policy

Purpose
This document explains the rules for using school digital accounts at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students receive digital access for educational use and must handle their accounts responsibly.

Expected Use
Students may use school accounts for:
- accessing learning platforms
- email communication
- submitting assignments
- using approved educational tools
- receiving official school information

Student Responsibility
Students must:
- keep login credentials private
- use strong passwords
- log out from shared devices
- avoid misuse of school systems
- report suspected account misuse promptly

Prohibited Use
Students may not:
- share accounts with others
- attempt unauthorized access
- use school systems for harmful or illegal activity
- send inappropriate or abusive content through school channels

Consequences
Misuse of digital accounts may lead to restricted access, disciplinary follow-up, or other school measures.

Contact
Questions about digital account use should be directed to the ICT helpdesk or student administration.
