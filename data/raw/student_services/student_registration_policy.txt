Student Registration Policy

Purpose
This document explains the registration rules for students at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
A student must be properly registered in order to participate in classes, assessments, guidance, and other official program activities.

Registration Requirements
Students may be required to provide:
- personal identification details
- previous education information where applicable
- enrollment forms
- any required supporting documentation

Start of Participation
A student may only officially begin the program after registration has been confirmed by the school.

Student Responsibility
Students are responsible for:
- providing accurate information
- checking registration status
- responding to requests for missing documents
- keeping contact details up to date

Incomplete Registration
If registration is incomplete:
- access to systems may be delayed
- participation in classes or exams may be restricted
- additional administrative follow-up may be required

Contact
Questions about registration should be directed to student administration.
