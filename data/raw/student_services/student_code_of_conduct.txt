Student Code of Conduct

Purpose
This document explains the expected behavior of students at Nova Tech College.

General Principle
Students are expected to contribute to a respectful, safe, and professional learning environment.

Expected Behavior
Students must:
- treat others with respect
- follow instructions from staff
- behave honestly
- use school facilities responsibly
- avoid behavior that disrupts learning or safety

Unacceptable Behavior
Unacceptable behavior may include:
- bullying
- discrimination
- intimidation
- aggression
- repeated disruption
- misuse of school systems or facilities

Professional Conduct
Because Nova Tech College prepares students for professional practice, respectful communication, reliability, and responsibility are considered important parts of student behavior.

Consequences
Behavior that violates the code of conduct may lead to warnings, meetings, support interventions, or other institutional measures.

Contact
Questions about student conduct should be directed to the academic mentor or student administration.
