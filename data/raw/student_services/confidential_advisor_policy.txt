Confidential Advisor Policy

Purpose
This document explains the role of the confidential advisor at Nova Tech College.

Role of the Confidential Advisor
A confidential advisor provides a safe first point of contact for students who want to discuss sensitive issues in confidence.

Possible Topics
Students may contact the confidential advisor regarding:
- intimidation
- bullying
- discrimination
- harassment
- unsafe behavior
- integrity concerns
- other sensitive personal situations related to the school environment

Support Function
The confidential advisor can:
- listen to the student’s concerns
- explain possible next steps
- help the student understand available options
- refer the student to the correct process or support service

Important Note
The confidential advisor is not the same as a disciplinary authority and does not automatically make formal decisions.

Contact
Students who need confidential support should contact the confidential advisor through the official school contact information.
