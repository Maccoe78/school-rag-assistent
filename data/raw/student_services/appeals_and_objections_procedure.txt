Appeals and Objections Procedure

Purpose
This document explains how students can formally object to or appeal certain decisions at Nova Tech College.

General Principle
Students have the right to challenge specific formal decisions when they believe procedures were not followed correctly or the outcome is unreasonable.

Possible Topics
An objection or appeal may relate to:
- assessment decisions
- progression decisions
- disciplinary measures
- formal administrative decisions
- other official outcomes where a review route exists

First Step
Students are encouraged to first seek clarification from the relevant teacher, department, or office before starting a formal procedure.

Formal Submission
A formal objection or appeal should:
- be submitted within the applicable deadline
- clearly describe the decision being challenged
- explain the reason for the objection
- include relevant supporting information

Review Process
The school reviews the submission according to the applicable procedure and communicates the next steps or decision.

Contact
Questions about appeals and objections should be directed to the relevant administrative office or Exam Office.
