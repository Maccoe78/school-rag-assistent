Special Support Services

Purpose
This document explains the support available for students who need additional assistance at Nova Tech College.

General Principle
The school aims to provide accessible support for students who face barriers that may affect participation or study success.

Examples of Support Needs
Support may be relevant for students with:
- dyslexia
- concentration difficulties
- physical limitations
- mental health challenges
- temporary personal difficulties
- other documented support needs

Possible Support Arrangements
Depending on the case, support may include:
- study planning guidance
- adapted communication
- practical arrangements where possible
- referral to internal or external support resources
- coordination with teachers where appropriate

Confidentiality
Personal circumstances are handled carefully and only shared when necessary for providing support.

Student Responsibility
Students are encouraged to contact the school early if support may be needed. Some arrangements may require documentation or formal discussion.

Contact
Questions about special support should be directed to student support services.
