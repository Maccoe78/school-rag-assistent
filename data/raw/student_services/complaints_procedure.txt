Complaints Procedure

Purpose
This document explains how students can submit a complaint at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students have the right to raise concerns about education, supervision, communication, behavior, or organizational matters.

Examples of Complaints
A complaint may relate to:
- teaching quality
- unclear communication
- unfair treatment
- inappropriate behavior
- administrative problems
- failure to follow agreed procedures

Recommended First Step
Where possible, students are encouraged to first discuss the issue with the person or department involved.

Formal Complaint
If the issue is not resolved informally, the student may submit a formal complaint through the school’s official process.

Complaint Requirements
A complaint should include:
- a clear description of the issue
- relevant dates or context
- names of involved roles if applicable
- any supporting information

Handling Process
The school reviews the complaint and responds according to internal procedures. Additional information may be requested.

Contact
Questions about complaints should be directed to student administration or the complaints contact point.
