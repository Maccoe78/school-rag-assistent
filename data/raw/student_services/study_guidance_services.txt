Study Guidance Services

Purpose
This document explains the study guidance available to students at Nova Tech College.

General Principle
Nova Tech College offers study guidance to help students manage progress, planning, challenges, and educational choices.

Forms of Guidance
Guidance may include:
- academic mentoring
- study planning support
- progress discussions
- referral to additional services
- advice on balancing workload and deadlines

When Guidance May Help
Students are encouraged to seek guidance when they:
- struggle with planning
- fall behind in coursework
- experience stress related to study
- are unsure about expectations
- need support in making academic decisions

Student Responsibility
Students are expected to:
- attend scheduled guidance meetings
- communicate concerns honestly
- follow up on agreed action points
- ask for help in time

Contact
Questions about study guidance should be directed to the academic mentor.
