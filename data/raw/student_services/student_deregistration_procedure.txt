Student Deregistration Procedure

Purpose
This document explains how students can leave or stop their program at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
A student who wants to stop studying must follow the official deregistration procedure. Stopping attendance without formal deregistration does not automatically end enrollment.

Possible Reasons for Deregistration
Reasons may include:
- personal circumstances
- transfer to another program
- health-related issues
- change in career direction
- financial or administrative reasons

Procedure
The student must:
- contact student administration
- complete any required forms
- discuss the situation if requested
- return school property if applicable

Important Note
Before deregistration is finalized, the school may provide information about alternatives such as support, pause options, or study guidance.

Consequences
Deregistration may affect:
- access to school systems
- study progress
- internship planning
- future re-enrollment conditions

Contact
Questions about deregistration should be directed to student administration.
