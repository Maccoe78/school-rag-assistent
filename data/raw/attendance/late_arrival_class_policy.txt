Late Arrival Class Policy

Purpose
This document explains the rules for arriving late to lessons and scheduled educational activities at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students are expected to be on time for all lessons, workshops, project meetings, and coaching sessions.

Impact of Late Arrival
Late arrival can:
- interrupt the learning environment
- reduce participation quality
- affect group work
- lead to missed instructions or safety briefings

Teacher Discretion
A teacher may decide whether a late-arriving student can still participate immediately, especially in:
- practical lessons
- labs
- workshops
- presentations
- assessment-related activities

Repeated Late Arrival
Repeated lateness may be treated as an attendance concern and may lead to:
- warning conversations
- registration in the attendance system
- mentor intervention
- consequences for professional behavior assessment

Student Responsibility
Students must:
- plan travel and preparation time properly
- notify the school when significant delay is unavoidable
- enter respectfully and without disrupting the lesson if allowed

Exceptional Circumstances
Documented transport disruption or urgent circumstances may be taken into account, but this does not automatically cancel the lateness registration.

Contact
Questions about lateness should be directed to the teacher or academic mentor.
