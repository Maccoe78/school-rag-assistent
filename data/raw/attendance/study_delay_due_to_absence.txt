Study Delay Due to Absence

Purpose
This document explains how absence can affect study progress at Nova Tech College.

General Principle
Frequent or long-term absence can lead to delays in completing coursework, assessments, projects, internships, or graduation requirements.

Possible Effects of Study Delay
Study delay may result in:
- missing deadlines
- incomplete assignments
- postponed exams
- delayed internship completion
- inability to progress to the next period
- later graduation

Shared Responsibility
The student is responsible for attendance and timely communication. The school is responsible for offering guidance within reasonable limits.

Prevention
Students should contact the school early if absence begins to affect:
- assignment completion
- exam preparation
- attendance requirements
- internship obligations
- personal study planning

Support Options
Possible support may include:
- mentor guidance
- study planning adjustments
- referral to student services
- discussion of realistic deadlines where policy allows

Limitations
Support does not guarantee exemption from academic standards. Some missed components must still be completed before progress can continue.

Contact
Questions about study delay should be directed to the academic mentor or student support services.
