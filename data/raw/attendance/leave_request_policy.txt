Leave Request Policy

Purpose
This document explains how students can request leave for special circumstances at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students are expected to attend all scheduled educational activities. Leave is only granted in exceptional or important circumstances.

Possible Reasons for Leave
Examples may include:
- family events of major importance
- legal obligations
- urgent personal circumstances
- religious observances
- special situations approved by the school

Non-Valid Reasons
Leave is generally not granted for:
- vacations outside official holiday periods
- social plans
- convenience-related scheduling issues
- avoidable travel arrangements

Request Procedure
- Leave must be requested in advance whenever possible.
- The request must be submitted through the official school process.
- The student must clearly explain the reason, date, and duration of the requested leave.
- Supporting documentation may be required.

Decision Process
The school reviews each request individually. Approval depends on:
- the reason
- the timing
- the student’s attendance history
- whether important assessments or mandatory activities are affected

Student Responsibility
A leave request is not valid until formally approved. Students remain responsible for attendance until approval is confirmed.

Contact
Questions about leave requests should be directed to student administration or the academic mentor.
