Sick Report Procedure

Purpose
This document explains how students must report illness at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
If a student is unable to attend classes, exams, internships, or other required activities due to illness, the student must report sick as soon as possible.

When to Report
- Illness should be reported before the start of the scheduled activity whenever possible.
- If this is not possible, the student must report the illness as soon as circumstances allow.
- Multi-day illness may require follow-up communication.

How to Report
Students must report illness through the official channel, such as:
- the student portal
- the designated absence reporting system
- the assigned school contact point

The student must not rely on classmates to report illness on their behalf unless this is unavoidable in exceptional circumstances.

Required Information
A sick report should include:
- full name
- student number
- date of absence
- expected duration if known
- whether an exam, internship, or mandatory activity is affected

Longer Illness
If the illness lasts several days or affects study progress significantly, the student may be asked to contact:
- the academic mentor
- student support services
- internship supervision staff if stage/BPV is involved

False or Improper Reporting
Improper use of the sick report procedure may be treated as unauthorized absence.

Contact
Questions about reporting illness should be directed to student administration.
