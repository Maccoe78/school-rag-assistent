Attendance Policy

Purpose
This document explains the general attendance rules at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Students are expected to attend all scheduled lessons, workshops, project sessions, coaching meetings, and other required educational activities. Attendance is considered an important part of study progress and professional development.

Minimum Participation
- Students must attend all mandatory educational activities unless stated otherwise.
- Some modules may set a minimum attendance percentage.
- Repeated absence can affect study progress, practical assessments, and eligibility for certain course components.

Student Responsibility
Students are responsible for:
- checking their timetable regularly
- arriving on time
- actively participating in class
- reporting absence correctly
- catching up on missed work independently

School Responsibility
Nova Tech College is responsible for:
- communicating schedules clearly
- recording attendance consistently
- informing students when attendance becomes a concern
- offering guidance where absence affects progress

Mandatory Activities
Activities that may be mandatory include:
- practical lessons
- labs
- project presentations
- coaching meetings
- assessment briefings
- exams and exam preparation sessions

Repeated Absence
Frequent absence may lead to:
- a warning
- a meeting with a mentor
- extra study guidance
- referral to support services
- consequences for progress or participation in certain activities

Exceptional Circumstances
Students facing medical, personal, or family-related difficulties should report this as soon as possible so that support can be discussed.

Contact
Questions about attendance should be directed to the academic mentor or student administration.
