Online Class Attendance

Purpose
This document describes attendance expectations for online classes at Nova Tech College.

General Rule
Attendance requirements also apply to online lessons, meetings, and digital learning activities when these are part of the official program.

Expected Participation
Students are expected to:
- join on time
- remain present for the required duration
- participate actively when asked
- use school-approved platforms where required
- follow instructions from the teacher

Technical Readiness
Students are responsible for:
- having a working internet connection where reasonably possible
- using the correct login details
- testing audio and microphone access if needed
- reporting technical issues promptly

Camera and Microphone Use
Rules for camera or microphone use depend on the nature of the class. Teachers may set participation expectations for presentations, discussions, or supervision sessions.

Technical Problems
If attendance is affected by technical issues, the student must report this as soon as possible. Repeated technical issues may require consultation with support staff.

Unauthorized Online Absence
Logging in without meaningful participation may still be treated as absence if the student is not actually engaged in the lesson.

Contact
Questions about online attendance should be directed to the course teacher or academic mentor.
