Absence During Exam Period

Purpose
This document explains the special rules that apply when a student is absent during exam periods or formal assessment weeks.

General Rule
Students are expected to remain available during scheduled exam periods. Absence during these periods is taken seriously because it may directly affect study progress.

What Counts as Exam Period
Exam period may include:
- written exam weeks
- practical assessment blocks
- portfolio deadlines
- oral exam scheduling windows
- final project assessment periods

Reporting Absence
Absence during an exam period must be reported immediately using the official reporting procedure. Additional documentation may be required.

Consequences
Depending on the situation, absence during exam periods may lead to:
- missed assessment opportunities
- delayed grading
- resit requirement
- postponement of progression decisions

Valid Circumstances
Illness, emergencies, or approved leave may be accepted if reported properly and supported where required.

Student Responsibility
Students must avoid planning personal travel or optional appointments during official exam periods unless explicitly approved.

Contact
Questions about absence during exam periods should be directed to the Exam Office.
